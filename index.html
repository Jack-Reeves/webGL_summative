Instructions:

Animations:

  • W: rotates the lower lamp arm
  • S: rotates the lower lamp arm
  • A: rotates the upper lamp arm
  • D: rotates the upper lamp arm
  • Q: rotates the base of the lamp
  • E: rotates the base of the lamp
  • O: opens the cupboard (hold)
  • P: closes the cupboard (hold)
  • K: opens the drawer (hold)
  • L: closes the drawer (hold)

Camera Controls:

  • 1: Toggle between camera move and pan
  • Up arrow: Pans the camera upwards / moves camera -ve in y-plane
  • Down arrow: Pans the camera upwards / moves camera +ve in y-plane
  • Left Arrow: Pans the camera to the left / moves camera -ve in x-plane
  • Right arrow: Pans the camera to the right / moves camera +ve in x-plane
  • Shift: Moves the camera +ve in the z-plane
  • Ctrl: Moves the camera -ve in the z-plane
